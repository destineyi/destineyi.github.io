Frequently Asked Questions

  • What documents do I need to bring for tax preparation?

    Generally, you'll need W-2s, 1099s, receipts for deductions, previous year's tax returns, and any other income or expense documentation. We'll provide a comprehensive checklist during our consultation to ensure you have everything needed.

  • How quickly can you complete my tax return?

    Turnaround times depend on the complexity of your situation and how organized your records are. Simple returns can often be completed within a week, while more complex cases may take longer. We'll provide a timeline during our initial consultation.

  • Can you help with business setup and structure?

    Yes! We provide guidance on business structure, entity selection, and initial setup. We can help you choose the best structure for your business goals and ensure proper compliance from the start.
